<b><color=magenta>Magnet</color></b>: Puck is magnetized towards your stick if it's close
<b><color=cyan>Lasso</color></b>: Pulls puck towards you quickly
<b><color=purple>Rage</color></b>: Knocks down anyone you touch for 7 seconds
<b><color=teal>Grapple</color></b>: Launches you towards the puck
<b><color=orange>Punch</color></b>: Pushes the puck forward
<b><color=yellow>Glue</color></b>: Puck attaches to your stick for 2 seconds
<b>Double tap 1 to use your powerup, and 1-2 to view your current powerup</b>
